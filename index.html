Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free?

PYCI's tools can be used at no cost to you! The goal is not profit but promoting financial education and transparency!

Is it just for students and youth?

While PYCI's target audience is youth, it can be used by anyone!

How is the PYCI Score generated?

Your PYCI score is generated by a variety of data collected from your financial history. More details about what that actally entails can be found in the About section where I go into detail about the process about my project!
